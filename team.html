About Athena's Warriors

Athena's Warriors is a robotics team dedicated to creating a diverse and passionate STEM workforce of both men and women. Students in Athena’s Warriors will be introduced to new concepts and information as they continue to explore both the engineering and business aspects of being on the team. Participating in Athena’s Warriors is equivalent to a college level engineering experience. Students will compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition each year along with community service projects made to educate the community on technology of the future.

Patrick Booth

Patrick is excited to return to FIRST for his sixth season as a technical mentor for the FRC Team 3182 - Athena’s Warriors, specializing in electronics and programming. He also volunteers at FRC competitions as a game announcer. Outside of FIRST, Patrick went to the University of Connecticut for a Bachelor's of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering, and Rensselaer Polytechnic Institue for a Master’s in Computer and Systems Engineering. He currently works for Pratt & Whitney, building jet engine simulations for pilot training and leading teams writing code for the engine controller. You can also find him running and cycling just about everywhere, and jumping around on stage in community theater musicals across the state (in the off season, of course…).

Twitter: @motleymentor

Renee Jurek

Renee Jurek is in her fifth season as a mentor for the team. She has been a mentor on the technical and non-technical aspects of the team with a primary focus on mechanical design. She has also volunteered at local FIRST Lego League competitions for the past two years. Renee graduated with a bachelor of science degree from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in 2007 and currently works for United Technologies Pratt & Whitney in the turbine durability group with a focus on aerodynamics and heat transfer. Aside from her passion of serving as a mentor on Athena’s Warriors, she also plays recreational league softball, volleyball and golf and enjoys playing guitar and reading books in her spare time.
