About Athena's Warriors

Athena's Warriors is a robotics team dedicated to creating a diverse and passionate STEM workforce of both men and women. Students in Athena’s Warriors will be introduced to new concepts and information as they continue to explore both the engineering and business aspects of being on the team. Participating in Athena’s Warriors is equivalent to a college level engineering experience. Students will compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition each year along with community service projects made to educate the community on technology of the future.

Patrick Booth

Patrick is excited for another season of awesome robot action with Athena’s Warriors! This season will be his fifth as a technical mentor for the team, specializing in electronics and programming. He also volunteers at FRC competitions as a game announcer. Outside of FIRST, Patrick went to the University of Connecticut for a Computer Science and Engineering degree, and Rensselaer Polytechnic Institue for a Master’s in Computer and Systems Engineering. He currently works for Pratt & Whitney, building jet engine simulations for pilot training. You can also find him running and cycling just about everywhere, and jumping around on stage in community theater musicals across the state (in the off season, of course…). Go Warriors!
